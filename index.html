Welcome!

Tristan is driven by the feeling of wonder the world is filled with. He is eager for knowledge and understanding, as well as a magical book to finally answer the trillion questions he ponders hourly.

As a programmer with more than 3 years of experience, he’s worked on many projects, such as Ceramic Soul, Flatline, Deimos, Ferret Fumble, and a slue of personal projects.

When he’s not completing schoolwork or managing production pipelines for his game team, you can find him spending time with his family, enjoying the feeling of wind on his longboard, or simply trying to get a handle on the tower of dishes that seemingly spawns daily.

His current project Ceramic Soul—a 3D puzzle horror game—is being curated by a team of 28 students.
